Terms and Conditions

1. Game Overview

Boggle Blitz Online is a multiplayer word game where players compete to find words in a grid of letters. The game is designed for entertainment and educational purposes only.

2. Privacy and Data

We take your privacy seriously:

  • No personal data is stored or tracked beyond the current game session
  • Nicknames are used only for game identification and are not stored
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • Game scores and words are not saved after the game ends

3. User Conduct

Players are expected to:

  • Use appropriate and non-offensive nicknames
  • Not use automated tools or bots
  • Not attempt to manipulate the game or other players
  • Respect other players and maintain good sportsmanship

4. Game Rules

  • Minimum of 2 players required to start a game
  • Maximum of 6 players per room
  • Words must be at least 3 letters long
  • Words must be formed using adjacent letters (including diagonals)
  • Letters cannot be reused in the same word
  • Duplicate words between players score 0 points
  • Scoring:
    • 3-4 letters: 1 point
    • 5 letters: 2 points
    • 6 letters: 3 points
    • 7 letters: 5 points
    • 8+ letters: 11 points

5. Technical Requirements

  • Modern web browser with JavaScript enabled
  • Stable internet connection
  • No special software or downloads required

6. Disclaimer

The developers of Boggle Blitz Online:

  • Are not responsible for any misuse of the game
  • Do not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free gameplay
  • Reserve the right to modify game rules and features
  • Are not liable for any technical issues or data loss

7. Fair Play

To ensure fair play:

  • All word validation is performed server-side
  • No client-side scoring is allowed
  • Anti-cheat measures are in place
  • Players found violating rules may be banned
